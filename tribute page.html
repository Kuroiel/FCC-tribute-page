Emet-Selch

The Hero the First needs but doesn't deserve

Picture of Emet-Selch being Emet-selch

Emet-Selch has been priased as one of the best villans to ever grace the Final Fantasy series, on the level of Kefka. Yes, he is a bad guy but he is presented as not that much of a villan at the beginning of the story. He is content to let the main character go around the land and advance their agenda. In a way, this also advances Emet-Selch's agenda, depending on how the main character progresses. He even actually went out of his way to help the protagonist during their adventure in the First.

    Notable achievements
    • Founder of the Garlean Empire
    • Member of the Convocation of Fourteen
    • Engineered multiple Umbral Calamities upon the Source
    • Rescued the MC's companion from the Aetherstream by snapping his fingers
